Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our accounting, tax, and business consulting services.

  • What services do you offer?
    We provide accounting, bookkeeping, tax preparation, business valuation, M&A advisory, and consulting services tailored for small businesses and entrepreneurs.
  • Who are your typical clients?
    Our clients are small business owners, startups, and entrepreneurs across various industries, primarily in Los Angeles and the surrounding areas.
  • Do you work with businesses outside of Los Angeles?
    Yes! We serve clients throughout the United States, both in-person and remotely.
  • How do I get started?
    Simply contact us for a free consultation. We'll discuss your needs and recommend the best services for your business.
  • What accounting software do you support?
    We work with QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks, Wave, and other leading accounting platforms.
  • Can you help with tax planning and filing?
    Absolutely. We offer year-round tax planning and preparation services to help you minimize your tax liability and stay compliant.
  • Do you offer one-time projects or ongoing support?
    Both! We provide ad-hoc services for specific needs as well as monthly, quarterly, and annual support packages.
  • How are your fees structured?
    Our fees depend on the scope and complexity of the services you need. We offer transparent, competitive pricing and will provide a detailed quote after your consultation.
  • Is my business information kept confidential?
    Yes. We take client confidentiality seriously and use secure systems to protect your sensitive information.
