3 Helpful Business Calculators Every Entrepreneur Should Use

Save time and make smarter decisions with these essential calculators for small business owners: Break-Even, Profit Margin, and Loan Payment.

1. Break-Even Calculator

Find out how many sales you need to cover your costs and start making a profit.

Break-Even Point (Units) = Fixed Costs / (Unit Price - Variable Cost per Unit)

2. Profit Margin Calculator

Quickly calculate your profit margin to understand your business’s profitability.

Profit Margin (%) = [(Revenue - Cost) / Revenue] × 100

3. Loan Payment Calculator

Estimate your monthly loan payment to plan your business finances.

Monthly Payment = [P × r × (1 + r)n] / [(1 + r)n - 1]
